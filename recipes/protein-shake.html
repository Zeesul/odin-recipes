Triple Berry Protein Smoothie

Description

Refreshing smoothie to make at any time of the day! This Triple Berry Smoothie is packed with clean ingredients and a whopping 12g of protein per serving.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups frozen triple berry mix
  • 1 medium frozen banana
  • 1/2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup vanilla protein powder
  • 1.25 cups unsweetened almond milk

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a high-speed blender and mix until smooth.
